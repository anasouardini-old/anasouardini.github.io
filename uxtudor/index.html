About Us

According to the Teachers Registration Council of Nigeria, Nigeria needs at least 250,000 teachers annually to cater for the growing population of pupils.

  This is what birthed the idea of giveatech.org; to grow a community of volunteer teachers who are willing to teach children in low resource schools and communities at their spare time.
